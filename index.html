PACMAN

SCORE: 0

  • - Pacman needs to eat as many pac dots (1 point) and power pellets (10 points) as possible.
  • - Pacman needs to avoid getting caught by 4 ghosts: Blinky, Pinky, Inky and Clyde.
  • - If Pacman eats a power pellet, ghosts will blink for 10 seconds, and Pacman has the chance to eat them (100 points).
  • - Game is over once Pacman is caught by one of the ghosts.
  • - Needs to score 300 or higher to win!
